Our Process

We believe the best results come from a clear process and open communication.
That’s why we follow a structured, step-by-step approach for every project, whether it's branding, design, a Webflow build, SEO, or all of it together.
01

Discovery Session

Let’s kick things off with a relaxed yet focused meeting where we dive into your goals, ideas, and timelines. It’s all about understanding your vision and figuring out the best way forward.

Duration: 1 Hour

02

Strategy & Planning

We organize your project structure, sitemap, and content needs. This step ensures we’re aligned on functionality, flow, and user experience before design begins.

Duration: 1–2 Days

03

UI Design

We design every screen and interaction with clarity and creativity—tailored to your audience, brand tone, and business goals. You’ll see exactly how the final site will look and feel.

Duration: 1–2 Weeks

04

Webflow Development

We bring the designs to life with clean, scalable Webflow development. The site will be responsive, SEO-optimized, and easy for you to manage.

Duration: 1–2 Weeks

05

Launch & Handoff

After QA and final checks, we launch your site. You’ll get full access, training if needed, and the option for ongoing support or future updates.

Duration: 1 Hour

Discovery Call (Free & No Commitment)

Goal: Understand your vision, business goals, target audience, and challenges.
We do:
  • Listen to your ideas and needs
  • Ask targeted questions about your current site, competitors, and success metrics
You receive:
  • Project brief summary
  • Rough timeline & investment estimate
Your role:
  • Share any inspiration, existing branding, content, or main points.

Strategy & Planning

Goal: Create a solid foundation so the project is built right from the start.
We do:
  • Research your industry and competitors
  • Map out site structure and user journeys
  • Define technical requirements (CMS, integrations, etc.)
You receive:
  • Detailed sitemap & wireframes (low-fidelity)
  • Content strategy outline
  • Finalized project timeline & scope
Your role:
  • Review and approve the plan (usually 1–2 rounds of feedback).

Design Phase

Goal: Bring your brand to life visually.
We do:
  • Create high-fidelity mockups (in Figma or Webflow)
  • Design key pages + mobile versions
  • Add interactive prototypes for animations and flows
You receive:
  • Full visual presentation of the design
  • Style guide (colors, typography, buttons, etc.)
Your role:
  • Provide feedback – we include up to 2 rounds of revisions.

Development & Build

Goal: Turn the approved design into a fully functional website.
We do:
  • Build everything in Webflow (responsive layouts, animations, CMS)
  • Integrate third-party tools (payments, forms, analytics, etc.)
  • Optimize for speed, SEO, and accessibility
You receive:
  • Staging site link for review
  • Weekly progress updates
Your role:
  • Test the staging site and share any final tweaks.

Testing & Refinement

Goal: Ensure everything works perfectly across devices and browsers.
We do:
  • Cross-browser & device testing (desktop, tablet, mobile)
  • Speed & performance audits
  • Accessibility checks (WCAG basics)
  • Fix any bugs or inconsistencies
You receive:
  • Final staging review link
  • Performance report summary
Your role:
  • Final approval – sign off when you're happy.

Launch

Goal: Go live with confidence.
We do:
  • Migrate content & set up domain/SSL
  • Configure analytics & tracking
  • Perform final launch checks
You receive:
  • Live website
  • Training session (live or recorded video) on how to edit content
  • Handover document (logins, guides, backups)
Your role:
  • Celebrate the launch!

Aftercare & Growth

Goal: Support your success long after launch.
We do:
  • 30 days of free post-launch support (bug fixes, minor adjustments)
  • Optional monthly maintenance & growth plan
  • Ongoing advice on content, SEO, and performance
You receive:
  • Priority support channel (Slack/email)
  • Monthly performance reports (if on maintenance plan)
Your role:
  • Keep running your business – we're here when you need us.
Simple & Direct

  • You stay in control – Regular reviews and approvals at every major milestone
  • We communicate clearly – Weekly updates and easy channels (Slack, email, Google meet)
  • Results-driven – Every step is focused on helping your business grow online
