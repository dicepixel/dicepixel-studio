Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about how we work and what’s included.
How do you decide the price?
We look at what you need — how many pages, what features (like CMS or e-commerce), and how much support — then give you a clear price based on that.
Why don’t you show fixed prices?
Because every project is different. We create custom quotes so you only pay for what you actually need — nothing more.
Can I get a price idea before a call?
Yes! Just fill out our contact form with a few details, and we’ll give you a rough estimate before we talk.
How long will my website take?
Most websites take 2 to 6 weeks, depending on the size and features. We’ll give you a full timeline after we understand your needs.
Do you help after the website is live?
Yes! We include support after launch and offer ongoing help if you want us to take care of your site long-term.
Can I make changes during the project?
Yes, no problem! If your plans change, we’ll update the scope and let you know how it affects the time and cost.
