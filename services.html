Frequently Asked Questions

We’ve answered common questions about our Webflow services, process, and what working with DicePixel looks like.
What type of clients do you work with?
We work with startups, growing businesses, and established brands that need high-performing Webflow websites. Most of our clients value clean design, interactive user experiences, strong SEO foundations, and scalable CMS architecture.
Do you only work with Webflow?
Webflow is our build platform of choice, but our work starts earlier, with brand identity, design and SEO strategy. We can take on branding or SEO projects on their own, or deliver the full journey end to end.
Can I hire you for just branding or just SEO?  
Absolutely. Plenty of clients come to us for one thing, a brand identity, an SEO foundation, a redesign. We scope each service to stand on its own, and we'll tell you honestly if you'd get more value bundling them.
Can you redesign my existing website?
Absolutely. We redesign websites to improve user experience, visual clarity, performance, and conversion rates. Our redesign process includes UX improvements, modern design systems, and technical optimization.
Do you handle migrations from WordPress or Wix to Webflow?
Yes. We manage full website migrations from platforms like WordPress and Wix, or any platform, to Webflow. This includes SEO-safe redirects, content restructuring, performance improvements, and preserving search rankings during the transition.
Is SEO included, or is it a separate service?
Every site we build ships with solid technical SEO foundations. For ongoing ranking growth, we offer dedicated SEO as its own service — a one-time SEO Foundation and a monthly plan with full reporting.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
Yes. We provide ongoing Webflow support including content updates, performance monitoring, SEO improvements, and continuous optimization to ensure your website evolves with your business.
Will I be able to manage the website myself?
Yes. We build user-friendly CMS structures inside Webflow, allowing you to update content, blog posts, and dynamic pages easily. We also provide guidance or training if needed.
How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines depend on scope and complexity. Most Webflow projects take between 3–6 weeks, including strategy, design, development, testing, and launch preparation.
Do you use animations on every project?
We implement interactive motion and micro-interactions where they enhance user experience and engagement. Our approach balances creativity with performance to ensure animations support usability—not distract from it.
How do we get started?
You can start by scheduling a consultation. We’ll discuss your goals, define scope, outline strategy, and provide a clear roadmap for building a high-performing Webflow website.
